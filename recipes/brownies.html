Decadent Brownies

A step by step on how to make the most delicious brownies you'll ever indulge in!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of warm butter
  • 200 grams of chocolate
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla
  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 cup of dutch processed chocolate powder
  • 1 teaspoon of salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees and melt the chocolate slowly in 30 second increments in the microwave
  2. Add melted chocolate into the butter and set aside
  3. Combine the eggs with the sugar and beat until fluffy, then add the rest of the dry ingredients
  4. Add the vanilla and melted butter and chocolate into the mixture, combine and then put into a lined baking sheet
  5. Bake for 25-35 minutes. Remove from oven and let cool for 30 minutes before indulging.
